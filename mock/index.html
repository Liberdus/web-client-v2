The live client should read `GET dao/proposals`, `GET dao/proposals/:id`, `GET dao/proposals/meta`, and `GET dao/voters/:proposalId`. Phase 1 writes map to `dao_proposal_create`, `dao_committee_vote`, `dao_committee_result`, `dao_vote`, `dao_vote_result`, `dao_claim_reward`, `dao_apply_parameters`, and `dao_burn_reward`.
